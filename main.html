Flowers photos

Flowers are the reproductive structures of flowering plants (angiosperms). They come in a breathtaking variety of shapes, sizes, and colors, serving not only to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies but also to brighten the natural landscape. From the delicate cherry blossom to the robust sunflower, flowers are a symbol of growth and beauty across the globe.
